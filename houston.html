Clutch City

Houston is known for its diverse culture and for its food. The city is known to attract people who work in the energy sector due to all the oil, gas, and chemical companies that are located here.

City Facts Information

Houston's annual median household income was $53,600, roughly $13,000 less than the state average of $66,963. Houston is on the lower end of household income when compared to other cities in the state.

Lee and Joe Jamail Skatepark

Lee and Joe Jamail Skatepark is one the biggest Skateparks in the state and is a place where many young kids and teens go to enjoy skateboarding.
