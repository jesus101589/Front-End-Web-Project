There's no place like Dallas

Dallas is home to the Dallas Cowboys which some call America's football team. The city is known for hosting many big businesses.

City Facts Information
Population 1,300,092
Founded November 1841
Region North Texas
Classification Urban

According to recent data, the average income in Dallas, Texas is generally higher than the rest of Texas, with the median household income in Dallas being around $63,985, while the statewide median income is lower at $66,963.
