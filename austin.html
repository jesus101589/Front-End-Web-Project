Life is a party in Austin

Austin is known for being the Silicon Valley of Texas and for good reason. Many technology companies from California have moved to central Texas due to cheaper real estate and for less government regulations.

City Facts Information
Population 984,567
Founded December 27th, 1839
Region Central Texas
Classification Urban

According to the US Census Bureau, the median household income in Austin, Texas is significantly higher than the rest of Texas, with Austin reporting a median household income of $86,556 compared to the state-wide median which is lower at around $66,963.

Lady Bird Lake

Lady Bird Lake in Austin is a unique attraction for paddle boarding. Located in the heart of the city, this stunning lake is always full of people enjoying their outdoor activities!
